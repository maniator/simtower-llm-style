# SimTower (1994) — Canonical Mechanics (per the GameFAQs FAQ)

**Source:** *SimTower FAQ/Walkthrough* by Kiwizoid (Kyle McDonald), v2.02, GameFAQs
(`gamefaqs.gamespot.com/pc/565191-simtower/faqs/28905`). Summarised here in our own
words as a parity reference — facts/numbers only, not the copyrighted text.

## Star ratings (the authoritative ladder)

| Rank | Requirements |
|---|---|
| 1★ | start |
| 2★ | population **300** |
| 3★ | population **1,000** + at least **1 Security office** |
| 4★ | population **5,000** + **more than one Hotel Suite** + **Recycling** demand met + **Medical** demand met + a **favorable VIP** rating |
| 5★ | population **10,000** + a **Metro Station** (underground) |
| TOWER | population **15,000** + a **Cathedral** (and a **wedding must occur** there, weekends only) |

Population = occupants (offices/condos/hotels). **Hotel guests count toward
population only while reaching 3★; once you are 3★ they no longer count.**
Offices pay rent at the start of each quarter (2 weekdays + 1 weekend per quarter).

## Transport rules
- A Sim takes at most **two** transport rides to a destination (hence sky-lobby transfers).
- Standard/service elevators span **≤30 floors**; **express has no length limit** but
  stops only at sky lobbies → express to 90, then a standard from 90→100.
- Housekeeping maids ride **service** elevators, not passenger elevators.

## Events
- **Rain:** fewer visitors (hurts fast food); usually clears before hotel check-in.
- **Fire:** spreads; offered **fire rescue for $500,000**, else let it burn and rebuild.
- **Terrorist:** blackmail **$300,000** or "find the bomb" (Security's 6 units/office
  sweep, one per floor); a missed bomb destroys ~5 floors.
- **VIP:** stays in a Hotel Suite; a favorable review is required for 4★; retryable.
- **Santa:** appears WE/4Q (cosmetic, no gift in the original).
- **Buried treasure:** building in the basement can unearth a chest worth **~$500,000**.

## Other
- Unclean hotel rooms breed **cockroaches** that spread down a hotel run (need enough
  housekeeping — one maid serves one floor; a unit covers ~6 floors via service lift).
- Office "**conditions are terrible**" = rent set too high (not a cleanliness issue).
