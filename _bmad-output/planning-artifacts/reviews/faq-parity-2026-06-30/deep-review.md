## 3. Must-Fix-Before-Merge vs Nice-to-Have

**Must fix before merge (blockers):**
1. Cockroach spread skipped at zero housekeeping — the new mechanic is inverted in its target scenario. (D1)
2. 5★/TOWER reachability — the game is plausibly unwinnable organically; reconcile `STAR_THRESHOLDS[5]` and add an organic win test. (D14)
3. Buried-treasure parking farm — money exploit worth ~27M; revert the parking-cost cut, gate treasure to usable/non-parking builds, or cap. (D10)
4. Emergency-choice modal lifecycle — pause the sim while a choice is open (or pre-resolve deterministically), and close/refresh the modal on auto-resolve so Pay can't no-op or charge the wrong amount. (D13)
5. Office-noise coefficient — drop below 0.05 so adjacency annoys rather than guarantees eviction/churn. (D25)
6. Persist `pending` across save/load — closes the free bomb-dodge exploit. (D24)

**Strongly recommended (same PR if cheap):**
- Make cockroach spread actually reach occupied/clean rooms (D12) and route around non-hotel neighbours (D7) — otherwise the mechanic is near-inert even after the D1 fix.
- TOWER should re-check operational metro (D6).
- Throttle the VIP "underwhelming" log line (D26).

**Nice-to-have / follow-up:**
- Reconcile the two parking-usability models (D29); pre-roll bomb RNG (D19); cinema economics tuning (D28); metro save-compat migration (D16); fix the stale census comment (D23); close the test gaps (D22/D30).
